Job Title: Admin Analyst – Internal Regulations
Department: DWS – Regulatory (Internal)
Reporting To: DWS Regulatory Lead 
Location: Nairobi
Employment Type: Full-Time
Role Purpose
The Regulatory Analyst – Internal Regulations supports DigitalQatalyst’s internal governance framework by managing, analyzing, and maintaining internal policies, guidelines, and risk controls. The role ensures that DQ’s internal regulatory ecosystem is structured, current, traceable, and consistently applied across studios and regions.
The role focuses on internal policy lifecycle management, risk tracking, governance documentation, and supporting DWS as the single source of truth for internal compliance materials.
Key Responsibilities
1. Internal Policy Monitoring & Analysis
Monitor, review, and analyze internal DQ policies, standards, guidelines, and governance frameworks.
Identify gaps, inconsistencies, or outdated internal regulatory content.
Assess operational risks arising from policy non-compliance or misalignment.
Document internal regulatory interpretations and policy positions clearly and consistently.
2. Policy & Governance Content Management
Draft, review, update, and maintain GHC Policies, Policy Sets, and Priority Guidelines.
Support the structuring, publishing, and version control of internal policies within the DQ Guidelines Marketplace.
Ensure all internal regulatory content aligns with approved governance frameworks and decision standards.
Maintain audit-ready documentation and traceability for all internal policies.
3. Risk Tracking & Compliance Operations
Maintain and update the DQ Risks Tracker for internal operational and governance risks.
Track mitigation actions, owners, and resolution timelines.
Support internal compliance reporting and audit-readiness processes.
Ensure consistent policy application across DQ studios and regions.
4. Internal Enablement & Communication
Translate internal policies into clear, actionable guidance for operational teams.
Support internal stakeholders with policy clarification and governance queries.
Disseminate policy updates through approved DWS communication channels.
5. Governance Process Execution & Integrity
Execute internal regulatory activities in strict alignment with DQ governance standards.
Ensure all policy decisions, updates, and interpretations are documented and traceable.
Uphold confidentiality, neutrality, and integrity in handling governance information.
Required Skills & Competencies
Core Skills
Strong policy analysis and documentation skills.
High attention to detail and process discipline.
Ability to structure and maintain governance documentation.
Strong written communication skills for formal policy content.
Behavioral & Professional Competencies
Process-driven, analytical, and detail-oriented.
Objective and disciplined in governance execution.
Reliable, accountable, and comfortable working within structured frameworks.
Qualifications & Experience
Bachelor’s degree in Compliance, Policy Analysis, Risk Management, Governance, Law, or a related field.
1+ year experience in internal compliance, policy management, governance, or risk-related roles.
Experience working with internal policy frameworks or governance systems is an added advantage.
