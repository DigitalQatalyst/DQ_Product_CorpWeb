Job Title: HR Analyst – Onboarding & People Development
Department: Human Resources
Reporting To: HRA Lead
Location: Nairobi
Employment Type: Full Time | On Site

Role Purpose
The HR Assistant – Onboarding & People Development supports the Human Resources function by delivering a smooth, engaging, and well-structured employee onboarding experience. The role focuses on effective communication, training coordination, and knowledge dissemination to ensure employees are well-equipped, informed, and aligned with organizational culture, policies, and performance expectations.

Key Responsibilities
1. Onboarding & Employee Integration
Coordinate and execute end-to-end onboarding for new hires, ensuring a positive and professional employee experience.
Prepare onboarding schedules, materials, welcome packs, and system access in collaboration with relevant teams.
Conduct onboarding and orientation sessions to familiarize new employees with company culture, policies, roles, and expectations.
Serve as the primary point of contact for new employees during their onboarding period.
2. Training & Development Support
Assist in the planning, coordination, and delivery of training and development programs.
Support training needs assessments and help organize learning sessions, workshops, and inductions.
Maintain training records, attendance registers, and learning progress reports.
Support the creation and updating of training materials, guides, and knowledge resources.
3. Knowledge Dissemination & Communication
Effectively communicate HR policies, procedures, and updates to employees in a clear and engaging manner.
Disseminate learning content and organizational knowledge across teams using appropriate channels.
Support internal communication initiatives related to learning, development, and employee engagement.
Ensure consistent understanding and application of HR practices across the organization.
4. HR Operations & Administrative Support
Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and onboarding documentation.
Support HR reporting related to onboarding, training participation, and employee development.
Assist with general HR administrative tasks as required, ensuring compliance and confidentiality.

Required Skills & Competencies
Core Skills
Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to explain concepts clearly.
Solid knowledge of onboarding processes, training principles, and employee development practices.
Ability to present information confidently to individuals and groups.
Strong organizational and coordination skills with attention to detail.
Proficiency in MS Office and HR systems (HRIS).
Behavioral & Professional Competencies
Highly communicative, approachable, and professional.
Knowledge-oriented with a passion for learning and sharing information.
Ability to disseminate knowledge effectively across diverse teams.
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to build trust with employees.
Proactive, reliable, and able to manage multiple priorities.
High level of discretion and confidentiality.

Qualifications & Experience
Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, Psychology, or a related field.
1–3 years of experience in an HR, onboarding, or training support role.
Exposure to onboarding programs and learning & development initiatives is an added advantage.
