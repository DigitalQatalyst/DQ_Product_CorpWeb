Job Title: Admin Analyst – External Regulations
Department: DWS – Regulatory Squad
Reporting To: DWS Regulatory Lead 
Location: Nairobi
Employment Type: Full-Time
No. of Positions: 3

Role Purpose
The Regulatory Analyst – External Regulations supports DigitalQatalyst’s compliance with external laws, regulations, and statutory requirements by monitoring regulatory developments and translating them into structured, actionable internal guidance.
The role ensures that DQ remains compliant across jurisdictions by providing timely regulatory intelligence, risk assessment, and documentation in support of scalable and legally sound operations.
Key Responsibilities
1. External Regulatory Monitoring & Analysis
Monitor, research, and track external laws, regulations, directives, and compliance requirements affecting DQ operations.
Identify regulatory changes and emerging legal risks across jurisdictions.
Analyze the impact of external regulations on DQ policies, operations, and governance frameworks.
Document regulatory interpretations in a structured and auditable format.
2. Regulatory Intelligence & Documentation
Maintain up-to-date regulatory registers and compliance references.
Draft regulatory summaries, advisories, and compliance guidance for internal stakeholders.
Support alignment of internal policies with applicable external regulatory requirements.
3. Risk Tracking & Compliance Operations
Update and maintain the DQ Risks Tracker for external regulatory and legal risks.
Track compliance obligations, mitigation actions, and regulatory deadlines.
Support compliance reporting, regulatory audits, and evidence collation.
Ensure consistent regulatory alignment across DQ regions and studios.
4. Regulatory Communication & Enablement
Translate complex legal and regulatory requirements into clear internal guidance.
Disseminate regulatory updates and compliance notices through approved DWS channels.
Support internal teams with regulatory clarification and compliance queries.
5. Governance Process Execution & Integrity
Execute all regulatory monitoring activities in line with DQ governance and compliance standards.
Ensure regulatory decisions and interpretations are traceable and audit-ready.
Maintain confidentiality, neutrality, and professional integrity at all times.
Required Skills & Competencies
Core Skills
Strong legal and regulatory research skills.
Ability to interpret laws and translate them into operational guidance.
Excellent documentation and written communication skills.
High attention to detail and regulatory accuracy.
Behavioral & Professional Competencies
Analytical, methodical, and risk-aware.
Objective and disciplined in regulatory interpretation.
Reliable, proactive, and able to work independently within defined frameworks.
Qualifications & Experience
Bachelor’s degree in Law, Regulatory Affairs, Compliance, Policy Analysis, or a related field.
1+ year experience in legal research, regulatory compliance, or external policy analysis.
Exposure to multi-jurisdictional regulatory monitoring is an added advantage.
